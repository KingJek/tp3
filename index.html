Slider-Style Mini Burgers

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) envelope onion soup mix
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 24 dinner rolls, split
  • ½ cup sliced pickles (Optional)

Kitchen Equipment

  • Oven
  • Baking Sheet
  • Measuring cups
  • Spatula
  • Knife

Directions

  1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cover a baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray.
  3. Mix together the ground beef and onion soup mix in a large skillet. Drain and discard any excess grease. Remove from heat.
  4. Cook and stir over medium-high heat until the beef is crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink.
  5. Stir the mayonnaise and Cheddar cheese into the ground beef mixture.
  6. Lay the bottoms of the dinner rolls on the prepared baking sheet. Replace the tops.
  7. Spread the cheese and beef mixture on the bottom half of each roll.
  8. Cover with another sheet of aluminum foil sprayed with cooking spray.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until the burgers are heated through and cheese melts, about 30 minutes. Serve with sliced pickles.
